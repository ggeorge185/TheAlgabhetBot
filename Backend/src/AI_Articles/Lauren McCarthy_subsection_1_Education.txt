McCarthy graduated from MIT with a Bachelor of Science|BS in Computer science|Computer Science and a BS in Art and Design. At MIT she studied technology's impact on physical interactions with her work ''Tools For Improved Social Interactions'', where she made an Anti-Daydreaming Device, a Happiness Hat, and a Body Contact Training Suit out of a knitted, wearable material.

McCarthy received her Master of Fine Arts|MFA degree from UCLA in 2011, where she has been an assistant professor since 2016.
