The Framework Programmes for Research and Technological Development|ISTAG advisory group suggests that society may be encouraged to use ambient intelligence, if AmI projects meet these criteria:

* Facilitate human contact.
* Be oriented towards community and cultural enhancement.
* Help to build knowledge and skills for work, better quality of work, citizenship, and consumer choice.
* Inspire trust and confidence.
* Be consistent with long-term sustainability &ndash; personal, societal, and environmental &ndash; and with lifelong learning.
* Be made easy to live with and are controllable by ordinary people.
