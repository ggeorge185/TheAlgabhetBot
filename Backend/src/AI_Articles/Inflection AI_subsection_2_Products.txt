The first product released widely by Inflection AI is a chatbot, Pi, named for “personal intelligence,” that is intended to function as an artificial intelligence-based personal assistant. Among the user experience goals that the company has stated for the Pi product are: providing an experience of emotional support for human users, in which the chatbot should be able to maintain an interactive text-based dialogue with a human user that includes elements of kindness, a diplomatic tone about sensitive topics, and Humour|humor. Comparisons and contrasts have been made between the Pi chatbot and ChatGPT, a chatbot created by OpenAI.
