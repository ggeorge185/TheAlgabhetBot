W Health Ventures is a venture capital firm established by Sunil Wadhwani and his family, dedicated to investing in and growing early-stage healthcare companies leveraging technology to address critical unmet healthcare needs. Operating in both the United States and India, W Health Ventures has supported companies such as BeatO, a platform specializing in diabetes management, Wysa, an AI-driven mental health chatbot, Jasper Health, a comprehensive cancer care platform; and Ryse Health, a provider of digital health solutions and others.
