In October 2021, the university launched an Executive Program  aimed at supporting decision makers to implement AI in their organizations. 

The MBZUAI Executive Program includes: 

*Pieter Abbeel – University of California, Berkeley|the University of California, Berkeley
*Sir J. Michael Brady – the University of Oxford
*Justine Cassell – Paris AI Research Institute (PRAIRIE)
*Michael I. Jordan – the University of California, Berkeley
*Tom M. Mitchell|Thomas Mitchell – Carnegie Mellon University
*David Parkes – Harvard University
*Daniela L. Rus|Daniela Rus – Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)

In March 2022, the university graduated the first class of Executive Program participants. A second class was announced in March 2022 by President Eric Xing.
