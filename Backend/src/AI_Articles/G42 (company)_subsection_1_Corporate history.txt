G42 was founded in 2018 and is based in Abu Dhabi, UAE. The company performs AI research and development processes on big data, AI, and machine learning via its subsidiary, the Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI). The company is chaired by the National Security Advisor of the UAE, Tahnoun bin Zayed Al Nahyan (national security advisor)|Tahnoun bin Zayed Al Nahyan, who is also its controlling shareholder. 
Peng Xiao is the Group CEO, former head of Emirati cybersecurity company DarkMatter.

In 2020, state-owned Mubadala Investment Company took a stake in the company, transferring ownership of two information technology companies, Injazat and Khazna to G42. The next year, American private equity firm Silver Lake (investment firm)|Silver Lake invested $800&nbsp;million for a minority stake.
