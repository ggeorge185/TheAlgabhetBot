Examples of automated theorem provers for first-order logic are:
* Prover9
* ACL2
* Vampire (theorem prover)|Vampire
Prover9 can be used in conjunction with the Mace4 Model checking|model checker. ACL2 is a theorem prover that can handle proofs by induction and is a descendant of the Boyer-Moore Theorem Prover, also known as Nqthm.
