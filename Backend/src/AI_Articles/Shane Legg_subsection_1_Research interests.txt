In the early 2000s, Legg re-introduced and popularized with Ben Goertzel the term "artificial general intelligence" (AGI), to describe an AI that can do practically any cognitive task a human can do. He said that at this time, talking about AGI "would put you on the lunatic fringe".

Legg is known for his concern of Existential risk from artificial general intelligence|existential risk from AI, highlighted in 2011 in an interview on ''LessWrong'' and in 2023 he signed the statement on AI risk of extinction
