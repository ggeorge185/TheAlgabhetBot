Whittaker is the co-founder and former faculty director of the AI Now Institute at NYU, a leading university institute dedicated to researching the social implications of artificial intelligence and related technologies which she started with Kate Crawford in 2017 after a symposium hosted by the White House. AI Now is partnered with the New York University Tandon School of Engineering, New York University Center for Data Science and Partnership on AI. They have produced annual reports that examine the social implications of artificial intelligence, including bias, rights and liberties.
