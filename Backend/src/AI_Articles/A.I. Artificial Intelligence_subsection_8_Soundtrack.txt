The film's soundtrack was released by Warner Sunset Records in 2001. The original Film score|score was composed and conducted by John Williams and featured singers Lara Fabian on two songs and Josh Groban on one. The film's score also had a limited release as an official "For your consideration Academy Promo", as well as a complete score issue by La-La Land Records in 2015. The band Ministry (band)|Ministry appears in the film playing the song "What About Us?" but the song does not appear on the official soundtrack album.
