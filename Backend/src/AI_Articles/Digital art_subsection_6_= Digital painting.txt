Digital painting mainly refers to the process of creating paintings on computer software based on computers or Graphics tablet|graphic tables. Through pixel simulation, digital brushes in digital software (see the software in Digital painting) can imitate traditional painting paints and tools, such as oil, acrylic acid, pastel, Charcoal (art)|charcoal, and airbrush. Users of the software can also customize the pixel size to achieve a unique visual effect (customized brushes).
