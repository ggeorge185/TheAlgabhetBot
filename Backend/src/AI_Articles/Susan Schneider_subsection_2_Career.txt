Schneider was an assistant professor of philosophy at the University of Pennsylvania and an associate professor of philosophy and cognitive science at the University of Connecticut. 
She was the founding director of the group for AI, Mind and Society ("AIMS"). In addition she has done research at the Australian National University, and at the Yale Interdisciplinary Center for Bioethics at Yale University In 2020, Schneider accepted the position of William F. Dietrich Professor of Philosophy at Florida Atlantic University (FAU), jointly appointed to the FAU Brain Institute.
