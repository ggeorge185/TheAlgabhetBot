The first book of the series, ''All Systems Red'', was published in May 2017. A sequel, ''Artificial Condition'', was released on May 8, 2018, followed by ''Rogue Protocol'' on August 7, 2018. The next installment, ''Exit Strategy'', was released on October 2, 2018. Wells noted in 2017 that the four novellas "do have an overarching story, with the fourth one bringing the arc to a conclusion." ''Fugitive Telemetry'', taking place chronologically between ''Exit Strategy'' and ''Network Effect'', was published in April 2021. A seventh novel, ''System Collapse'', was released on November 14, 2023.
