At the 88th Academy Awards, ''Ex Machina'' received a nomination for Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay|Best Original Screenplay and won Academy Award for Best Visual Effects|Best Visual Effects. The film's other nominations include five British Academy Film Awards, three Critics' Choice Movie Awards (winning one), and a Golden Globe Award.
