Wadhwani has led a majority investment into 2070 Health, a venture studio with the mission of building patient-centric healthcare companies from the ground up. This innovative venture is dedicated to transforming the healthcare industry by combining futuristic ideas, exceptional founders, and a robust innovation platform. The studio has raised funding to focus on identifying and addressing unmet healthcare needs, partnering with aspiring entrepreneurs, providing the necessary resources and support, and collaborating closely to develop groundbreaking solutions tailored to the needs of patients.
