CSAIL's research activities are organized around a number of semi-autonomous research groups, each of which is headed by one or more professors or research scientists. These groups are divided up into seven general areas of research:
* Artificial intelligence
* Computational biology
* Computer graphics|Graphics and Machine vision|vision
* Natural language processing|Language and Machine learning|learning
* Theory of computation
* Robotics
* Systems (includes computer architecture, databases, Distributed computing|distributed systems, Computer network|networks and networked systems, operating systems, programming methodology, and software engineering, among others)
