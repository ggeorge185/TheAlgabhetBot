Opta Index Limited was founded in 1996 to analyse Premier League football matches and was contracted by Sky Sports for their television broadcasts of the 1996–97 Premier League|1996–97 season. The 1997–98 Premier League|following season, Opta became the official statistics provider for the league itself and became sponsored by Carling.

The company was sold to Perform Group in 2013 for £40 million and remains part of Stats Perform.
