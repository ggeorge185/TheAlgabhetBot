On 20 April 2019, it was reported that several applications for Android (operating system)|Android devices developed by the subsidiary company, DO Global (formerly DU Group), were surreptitiously running revenue enhancing background programs on user devices since at least 2016. Just one of the apps, all of which were available on Google Play Store, had been downloaded 50 million times alone and carried a user rating of 4.5 stars by tens of thousands. DO Global was also banned from AdMob|Google's AdMob Network.
