Digital art can be purely computer-generated (such as Fractal|fractals and algorithmic art) or taken from other sources, such as a Image scanner|scanned photograph or an image drawn using vector graphics software using a Computer mouse|mouse or graphics tablet. Artworks are considered Digital painting|digital paintings when created similarly to non-digital Painting|paintings but using software on a computer platform and digitally outputting the resulting image as painted on canvas.

Amidst varied opinions on the pros and cons of digital technology on the arts, there seems to be a strong consensus within the digital art community that it has created a "vast expansion of the creative sphere", i.e., that it has greatly broadened the creative opportunities available to professional and non-professional artists alike.
