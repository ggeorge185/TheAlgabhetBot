The 2023 Netflix anime adaptation received positive reviews from critics. Ali Griffiths of ''Digital Spy'' said, "''Pluto'' sets a new gold standard for Netflix original anime." Devin Meenan of ''/Film|Slashfilm'' compared the complexity and characters to the celebrated graphic novel ''Watchmen''. Elijah Gonzalez  of ''Paste (magazine)|Paste'' magazine applauded the story's fresh takes on themes first introduced in Isaac Asimov's Three Laws of Robotics|laws of robotics. Joshua Rivera of ''Polygon (website)|Polygon'' called ''Pluto'' "one of the best sci-fi murder mysteries you can watch this year".
