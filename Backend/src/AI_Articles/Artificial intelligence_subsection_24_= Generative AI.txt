In the early 2020s, generative AI gained widespread prominence. ChatGPT, based on GPT-3, and other large language models, were tried by 14% of Americans adults. The increasing realism and ease-of-use of AI-based Text-to-image model|text-to-image generators such as Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion sparked a trend of Viral phenomenon|viral AI-generated photos. Widespread attention was gained by a fake photo of Pope Francis wearing a white puffer coat, the fictional arrest of Donald Trump, and a hoax of an attack on the The Pentagon|Pentagon, as well as the usage in professional creative arts.
