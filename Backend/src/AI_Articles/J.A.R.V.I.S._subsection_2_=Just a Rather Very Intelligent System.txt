The character makes his debut in the 2008 film ''Iron Man (2008 film)|Iron Man'' before subsequently appearing in the 2010 film ''Iron Man 2'', the 2012 film ''The Avengers (2012 film)|The Avengers'', and the 2013 film ''Iron Man 3''. J.A.R.V.I.S. is an AI that functions as Tony Stark (Marvel Cinematic Universe)|Tony Stark's assistant, running and taking care of all the internal systems of Stark's buildings and the Iron Man suits. In Peter David's novelization of ''Iron Man'', J.A.R.V.I.S. is said to be an acronym for "Just a Rather Very Intelligent System". J.A.R.V.I.S. also appears in the Disneyland attraction Innoventions (Disneyland)|Innoventions.
