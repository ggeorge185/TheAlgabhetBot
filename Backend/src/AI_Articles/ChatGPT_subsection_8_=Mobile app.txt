In May 2023, OpenAI launched an iOS app for ChatGPT. The app supports chat history syncing and voice input (using Whisper, OpenAI's speech recognition model).

In July 2023, OpenAI unveiled an Android (operating system)|Android app, initially rolling it out in Bangladesh, Brazil, India, and the United States|U.S. The app later became available worldwide. OpenAI is working on integrating ChatGPT with Android's assistant APIs.
