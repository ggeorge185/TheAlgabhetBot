Macrobinoculars are hand-held devices that function like binoculars, with the purpose of giving the user the ability to see vast distances. It was first seen on film in ''A New Hope'' and mentioned in its Star Wars: From the Adventures of Luke Skywalker|novelization. The websites tested.com reports that Sony has developed macrobinoculars comparable to the ones seen in ''Star Wars'', known as DEVs, and produced in separate types of models. These give the user the ability to see great distances clearly and record their sightings.
