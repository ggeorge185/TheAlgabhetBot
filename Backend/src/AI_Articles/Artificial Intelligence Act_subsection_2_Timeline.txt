In February 2020, the European Commission publishes "White Paper on Artificial Intelligence – A European approach to excellence and trust". In October 2020, a debates between EU leaders take place. On 21 April 2021, the AI Act is officially proposed. On 6 December 2022, the European Council adopts the general orientation, which allows negotiations to begin with the European Parliament. On 9 December 2023, after three days of “marathon" talks, the Council and Parliament concluded an agreement.

The AI Act is unlikely to take effect before 2025.
