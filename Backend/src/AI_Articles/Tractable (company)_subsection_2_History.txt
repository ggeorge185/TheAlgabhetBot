Alexandre Dalyac and Razvan Ranca founded Tractable in 2014, and Adrien Cohen joined as co-founder in 2015. The company employs more than 300 staff members, largely in the United Kingdom. It won the Best Technology Award in the 2020 British Insurance Awards.

In June 2021, Tractable announced a venture round that valued the company at $1 billion. Tractable was the UK's 100th billion-dollar tech company, or Unicorn (finance)|unicorn.

In July 2023, the company received a $65 million investment from SoftBank Group, through its SoftBank Vision Fund|Vision Fund 2.
